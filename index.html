Felicia Bisnath

/fəliːsjə bɪsnaθ/ | she/her

I am a first year PhD student of linguistics at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor advised by Dr. Savithry Namboodiripad. I am interested in contact effects in minoritised languages, specifically creoles and signed languages. My qualifying paper is a typological study of mouthings across signed languages.

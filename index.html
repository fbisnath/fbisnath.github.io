FELICIA BISNATH

/fɪliːsjə bɪsnaθ/ | she/her

I am PhD student of linguistics (beginning fall 2019) at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor advised by Dr. Savithry Namboodiripad and Dr. Natasha Abner. I have studied contact languages, specifically Caribbean creoles (Trinidadian French Creole, Sranan Tongo), and sign languages (Trinidad and Tobago Sign Language, Sign Language of the Netherlands). During my PhD I will continue to study sign languages and extend into gesture and language evolution. Download my CV below.

I am a third year PhD student of linguistics at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor advised by Dr. Savithry Namboodiripad and working with the Contact, Cognition and Change Lab. I'm interested in how language users recruit the semiotic resources available to them, e.g. words, gesture, during communication, and how that manifests itself cross-linguistically. I am specifically interested in contact effects in minoritised languages, specifically creoles and signed languages. My qualifying paper is a typological study of mouthings across 37 signed languages. See a preview of this study below!

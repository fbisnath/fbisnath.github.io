FELICIA BISNATH

/fəliːsjə bɪsnaθ/ | she/her

I am a first year PhD student of linguistics at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor advised by Dr. Savithry Namboodiripad. I have studied contact languages, specifically Caribbean creoles (Trinidadian French Creole, Sranan Tongo), and sign languages (Trinidad and Tobago Sign Language, Sign Language of the Netherlands). During my PhD I will continue to study sign languages and extend into gesture and language evolution. Download my CV below (last update: 18.01.2020).
